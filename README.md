# 🍽️ Food Waste Analytics

An AI and Data Analytics-driven platform designed to analyze food waste patterns, identify key causes of wastage, and generate actionable insights to promote sustainable food consumption and waste reduction.

The project leverages data analysis, visualization, and predictive techniques to help organizations, restaurants, households, and policymakers understand food waste trends and make informed decisions.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

Food waste is a major global challenge, contributing to economic losses, resource depletion, and environmental issues. Food Waste Analytics aims to transform raw food consumption and waste data into meaningful insights through analytics and visualization.

The system helps stakeholders monitor food waste, identify high-waste categories, predict future trends, and implement data-driven waste reduction strategies.

- Analyze food waste patterns.
